React-Native在OS X 10.11报错的问题

  • React-Native

前言

昨天晚上升级了Mac系统，结果发现在重新新建一个React-Native程序的时候竟然报错了

TypeError: Cannot read property 'root' of null at /Users/isasportas/Projects/canalplus/iOS/RemoteX1/node_modules/react-native/packager/react-packager/src/DependencyResolver/crawlers/index.js:16:84 at tryCallOne (/Users/isasportas/Projects/canalplus/iOS/RemoteX1/node_modules/react-native/node_modules/promise/lib/core.js:37:12) at /Users/isasportas/Projects/canalplus/iOS/RemoteX1/node_modules/react-native/node_modules/promise/lib/core.js:103:15 at flush (/Users/isasportas/Projects/canalplus/iOS/RemoteX1/node_modules/react-native/node_modules/promise/node_modules/asap/raw.js:50:29) at process._tickCallback (node.js:355:11)

产生原因

原来是由于brew安装的watchman的版本是3.1.0，但是改版本不支持mac的OS X 10.11系统

解决方案

卸载原来的watchman

brew uninstall watchman

安装最新版本的watchman

brew install watchman -HEAD
